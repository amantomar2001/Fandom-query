Base Game fucks[]

Without any DLCs, there are 3 playable Slugcats.

The banana[]

The Monk is a yellow Slugcat, and serves as the easy mode of Rain World. They are the sibling of The Survivor. Enemies are less aggressive and less common in the Monk's campaign.

The vanilla yogurt[]

The Survivor is a white Slugcat. They serve as the regular/normal mode of Rain World, with standard stats and spawns.

The strawberry[]

The Hunter is a light red Slugcat that serves as the hard mode of Rain World. Enemies are more common and more aggressive in the Hunter's campaign. The Hunter has tougher enemies, like Red Lizards and King Vultures. As opposed to regular campaigns, The Hunter's cycles count down, indicating it has limited time.

Downpour Slugcats[]

The Downpour DLC adds 5 new playable Slugcats.

The depressed boomy slugcat[]

The Artificer is a maroon Slugcat added in the Downpour DLC. They have the ability to turn Spears and rocks into explosives at the cost of no pips by holding grab, and can explode while in the air to double jump. Their campaign is locked until beating the game as either the Monk or The Survivor.

The Artificer permanently has the lowest possible reputation with monkeys and cannot gain more.

The fat fuck[]

The Gourmand is able to craft things by holding two objects in both of its hands and holding pick up to swallow them. There are many different combinations used in order to make different items. It can also jump onto creatures from a height to kill or hurt them due to their weight. On the other hand, however, it tends to get stuck in pipes and has a special mechanic called exhaustion. When exhausted, The Gourmand cannot move as quickly and doesn't jump as high.

The cocaine user[]

The Rivulet is a semi-aquatic light blue Slugcat added in Downpour. They are able to breathe longer underwater and move much faster, but cycles are much shorter and shelter failures are more common.

The spikey boi[]

The Spearmaster is a slugcat with no mouth that must eat by hitting creatures or popcorn plants with the white spears pulled from its tail. Once the creature it hits is dead, it no longer gains pips and must find another, living one. Unlike any other slugcat, it can hold two spears at once, though it cannot store one on its back.

The god incarnate[]

The Saint is a slugcat with a very long tongue it can use to grapple. The Saint is green, and unlike every other slugcat, fluffy. They are also a pacifist and cannot pick up spears, so they must run from fights.

Unlike the other slugcats, The Saint cannot throw spears or eat creatures, and has to escape predators using its tongue, and is physically unable to eat meat, spasming when doing so.

Slugcat Behaviors[]

Slugcats are idiots and generally lazy creatures. They can befriend other wild creatures such as Lizards by feeding them. They utilize spears and other objects in combat, with some even being able to craft objects such as lanterns, and explosives. Slugcats have an interesting relationship with the other morons they share a habitat with, the Scavengers. Some may become allies with scavengers, through trading and gifts, while others go out of their way to hunt down and kill scavengers whenever possible.

As well as being as intelligent as a rock, the slugcat is also a species of incredible fucked up autopsy, with one slugcat potentially looking almost nothing like the next. Although most slugcats vary only in color, some, The Spearmaster and Rivulet are sometimes shipped, they are animals. Most slugcats are bug and plant yum. though some are able to eat larger prey such as Lizards. Conversely, some have evolved in ways allowing only for the consumption of plant life.

Although the exact origin of slugcat is unknown, it is confirmed that they were originally created to clean out the insides of pipes.
